## What's a Converted Lead Worth to Credex?

Credex sells discounted AI infrastructure credits — Cursor, Claude, ChatGPT Enterprise, and similar — sourced from companies that overforecasted or pivoted. The discount relative to retail is real and substantial (assumption: 15–35% below list price based on the typical "overstock" discount in SaaS credit resale).

**Estimating LTV per converted customer:**

Assume a startup engineering team buying discounted AI credits through Credex:
- Average team size: 8 developers
- Average AI tool spend before audit: ~$400/month (based on interview data: ~$50/dev/month across Cursor + Claude or ChatGPT)
- Credex captures 10–20% gross margin on resold credits
- Customer stays with Credex for an average of 12 months before re-evaluating

| Input | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Monthly AI spend (pre-audit) | $400 |
| Estimated Credex credit purchase (40% of spend routed through Credex) | $160/month |
| Gross margin on credit resale | 15% |
| Gross profit per customer per month | $24 |
| Average customer lifetime | 12 months |
| **Estimated LTV per converted customer** | **~$288** |

This is a conservative estimate. For teams spending $1,000–$5,000/month (larger engineering orgs), LTV scales proportionally.

## What Is CAC at Each GTM Channel?

| Channel | Estimated CAC | Reasoning |
|---------|--------------|-----------|
| Hacker News Show HN (organic) | ~$0 | Zero paid spend. 40–60 audits from 200+ visitors. If 3–5 book a consultation and 1–2 convert to a credit buyer: CAC ≈ $0 direct cost. |
| X organic (own-audit post + DMs) | ~$0 direct, ~$20–$40 in time cost | 5–8 audits from DMs, 1–2 conversions. Time: ~4 hours of outreach at implied hourly rate. |
| SEO content (blog post) | ~$50–100 (writer time or tool cost) for long-tail content | Amortised over 12 months of organic traffic. If a "Cursor vs Copilot cost" post drives 50 visits/month and converts at 20% to audits, 1% of audits to Credex customers: ~1 customer/year per post. CAC from content: ~$75. |
| Product Hunt | ~$0 direct spend, significant time investment (~8 hours prep) | If 500 upvotes drive 1,000 visitors, 200 audits, 10 leads, 2 Credex customers: effective CAC ~$0. |
| Credex customer email (existing list) | Near $0 | Marginal cost of an email send. If Credex has 200 current customers and 20% run an audit, 10% of auditors book a consultation, 50% of those buy credits: 2 new upsell customers from the email. |

**Blended estimated CAC across all channels: $20–$75**

At a conservative LTV of $288 and blended CAC of $50, LTV:CAC is approximately **5.8:1** — healthy for a B2B lead-gen tool.

## What Would Have to Be True for $1M ARR in 18 Months?

$1M ARR from Credex credit margin means roughly **$1M / 15% gross margin = $6.7M in annual credit volume flowing through Credex**, attributable to AuditMyAI-sourced customers.

**Working backward:**

| Metric | Required number |
|--------|----------------|
| Annual Credex credit revenue (at 15% margin) | $6.7M |
| Average annual credit purchase per customer | $1,920 ($160/mo × 12) |
| Number of active Credex customers from AuditMyAI | ~3,500 |
| Monthly new customer acquisitions needed | ~195/month (to reach 3,500 in 18 months) |
| Audits needed per month (at 3.3% audit → customer conversion) | ~5,900 audits/month |
| Visitors needed per month (at 15% audit completion) | ~39,000/month |

**Is 39,000 monthly visitors achievable in 18 months?**

- A successful Product Hunt launch can drive 10,000–50,000 visitors in a week.
- A Hacker News front-page post drives 5,000–20,000 visitors in 48 hours.
- Two SEO articles ranking on page 1 for "AI tool cost comparison" could drive 5,000–15,000 monthly organic visits.
- Credex's existing sales motion (direct outreach, partnerships with AI-focused VCs and accelerators) can accelerate customer flow.

**Verdict:** $1M ARR in 18 months is ambitious but not impossible, contingent on: (1) at least one HN or PH moment that seeds organic word-of-mouth, (2) Credex dedicating a sales person to work the high-savings leads generated by the tool, and (3) the audit engine maintaining pricing accuracy so recommendations hold up under scrutiny. The biggest risk is not traffic — it's conversion at the consultation → purchase step, which depends entirely on Credex's sales execution.

**Rough inputs, explicit:**
- Gross margin assumption: 15% (could be as high as 30% for premium credits)
- Customer lifetime: 12 months (conservative; recurring infra spend tends to be sticky)
- Average monthly credit purchase: $160 (based on 40% of $400 monthly AI spend routed through Credex)
- All inputs are estimates. The math is illustrative, not a forecast.
