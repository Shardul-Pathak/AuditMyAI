## FAQ — 5 Real Q&As

**Q: Do I need to connect my billing accounts or credit cards?**  
A: No. You enter your tools, plans, and estimated monthly spend manually. We never touch your billing accounts. No OAuth, no integrations, no access to your payment data.

**Q: How accurate are the savings estimates?**  
A: Every pricing number in our audit engine traces to the official vendor pricing page, verified weekly. The savings logic is deterministic — we're not using AI to calculate numbers, we're using hardcoded rules that a finance person can read and verify. If a number looks wrong, we want to know.

**Q: What happens to my data after the audit?**  
A: Your audit is stored so you can share it or return to it via a unique URL. If you don't provide an email, we hold the data for 30 days and then delete it. If you do provide an email, we keep your audit record and email you if pricing changes affect your recommendations.

**Q: What does Credex do with my email?**  
A: If your audit shows significant savings (>$500/month), a Credex team member will reach out to explain how discounted AI credits could capture additional savings beyond what plan changes alone can deliver. You can opt out of this at any time. We never sell your data.

**Q: Is this actually free? What's the catch?**  
A: The audit is free, genuinely. Credex makes money when users with large AI spend choose to buy discounted credits through Credex. The audit is a lead-generation tool for Credex — we're transparent about that. If your savings are small or you're already optimised, we'll tell you honestly and not push anything on you.
