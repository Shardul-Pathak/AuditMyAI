# PRICING_DATA.md

All prices are in USD. Every number below traces to an official vendor pricing page. Verification dates are noted. Prices are subject to change — check official pages before relying on these figures.

---

## Cursor

- **Hobby (Free):** $0/month — limited Agent requests and Tab completions
- **Pro:** $20/month (or ~$16/month billed annually) — unlimited Tab completions, $20 monthly usage credits for premium models, unlimited Auto mode
- **Pro+:** $60/month — 3× usage credits compared to Pro
- **Ultra:** $200/month — 20× usage credits compared to Pro
- **Teams (Business):** $40/user/month — Pro-equivalent access + centralized billing, shared rules, admin dashboard, privacy mode, SSO
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing (contact sales) — pooled org usage, SOC 2, audit logs, invoice billing

Source: https://cursor.com/pricing — verified 2025-05-10

**Notes:** Since June 2025, Cursor moved from request-based limits to credit-based billing. The monthly subscription price equals the included credit pool in dollars (e.g. Pro = $20 credits/month). Auto mode is unlimited on all paid plans.

## GitHub Copilot

- **Free:** $0/month — up to 2,000 inline suggestions/month, limited chat
- **Pro:** $10/month — unlimited completions, premium model access, 300 premium requests/month included
- **Pro+:** $39/month — expanded premium request allowance, access to newer models
- **Business:** $19/user/month — includes $19 in monthly AI Credits, centralized billing, policy controls, IP indemnity, SSO (pooled credits from June 1, 2026)
- **Enterprise:** $39/user/month — includes $39 in monthly AI Credits, everything in Business plus private model fine-tuning, knowledge bases (GitHub Enterprise Cloud required)

Source: https://github.com/features/copilot/plans — verified 2025-05-10
Source: https://docs.github.com/en/copilot/concepts/billing/organizations-and-enterprises — verified 2025-05-10

**Notes:** From June 1, 2026, Copilot is transitioning to usage-based billing (token consumption model). Business and Enterprise seat prices remain unchanged but credits are now pooled at org level.

## Claude

- **Pro:** $20/month (or ~$17/month billed annually) — ~5× Free usage, Claude Code in terminal, unlimited Projects, Research feature, Google Workspace integration
- **Max 5×:** $100/month — 5× Pro usage per session, priority access to new features
- **Max 20×:** $200/month — 20× Pro usage per session

- **Team (Premium seat):** $100/user/month (annual) / $125/month (monthly) — 6.25× Pro usage per session, Claude Code access
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing — SSO/SCIM, audit logging, data residency, custom context windows, dedicated support

Source: https://claude.ai/upgrade — verified 2025-05-10

## ChatGPT / OpenAI (Subscription)

- **Free:** $0/month — access to GPT-5.3 Instant with rolling rate limits
- **Plus:** $20/month — higher limits, GPT-5.5 Thinking access, expanded image generation, Deep Research
- **Pro:** $200/month — unlimited high-performance access, 1M token context window, advanced reasoning
- **Business (formerly Team, renamed August 2025):** $20/user/month (billed annually) or $25/user/month (monthly), minimum 2 users — data privacy by default (no training on org data), admin controls, SAML SSO, SCIM, SOC 2 Type 2, shared GPTs
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing — everything in Business plus multi-region data residency, granular RBAC, full audit logs, 24/7 SLA support, dedicated AI advisor

Source: https://chatgpt.com/pricing — verified 2025-05-10
Source: https://openai.com/business/chatgpt-pricing/ — verified 2025-05-10
