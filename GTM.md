# GTM.md

## Who Is the Exact Target User

Not "startups." The specific target is an **engineering manager or CTO at a 5–30 person Series A company**, 6–18 months post-fundraise, who has recently onboarded 3–8 developers and is starting to feel the compounding cost of everyone picking their own AI tools independently.

They have a budget conversation coming up. Their CFO or finance lead has flagged that "AI subscriptions" is a growing line item and asked them to justify it. They've never done an audit before — they've just been approving individual tool requests without a framework.

Secondary target: **founder-engineers at pre-seed or seed stage** who wear both the product and the finance hat. They're paying for tools personally (or out of a tiny budget) and are acutely cost-conscious.

## How to Get the First 100 Users in 30 Days With $0 Budget

**Day 1–3: Hacker News Show HN.**  
Post "Show HN: Free tool to audit your AI tool spend." Engineering managers and founders are the core HN readership. The framing should lead with the honest utility ("I got tired of guessing whether my team was overpaying for Cursor vs Copilot") not marketing copy. A Show HN in the top 3 on the front page is worth 500–2,000 unique visitors in 48 hours. Target: 30–40 leads from this alone.

**Day 1–7: Post the result from your own team's audit.**  
Run an audit on a real AI stack (e.g. a 3-person engineering team using Cursor Pro + Claude Pro + ChatGPT Plus) and screenshot the results page showing ~$200/month in potential savings. Post to X with the title: "We just audited our AI tool spend. Here's what we found [with screenshot]." No link in the first tweet — put it in a reply or second tweet. Target: 500–1,000 impressions if you have even 100 relevant followers.

**Day 3–7: DM outreach on X to engineering managers and founders who've tweeted about AI tool costs.**  
Search X for "cursor pricing," "copilot cost," "claude team plan" — people who've tweeted about this in the last 2 weeks are in exactly the right moment. DM with: "Built a free tool that audits AI spend — would you try it on your stack and tell me if the recommendations make sense?" Target: 10–15 personal conversations, 5–8 completed audits.

**Day 7–14: SEO content.**  
Write a 1,500-word post: "Cursor vs GitHub Copilot vs Windsurf: Cost Per Developer in 2025." This is the exact query the target user is Googling. The post itself is useful without the tool; the tool is the CTA at the end. Publish on the project's subdomain blog (or dev.to for domain authority bootstrap). Target: 30–50 organic visitors within 30 days via long-tail.

**Day 14–30: Product Hunt launch.**  
Schedule a formal PH launch for week 3. Hunter should be someone with 500+ followers. Build a "notify me on launch" list from HN and X engagement beforehand. Target: top 5 product of the day, 200+ upvotes, 50+ additional leads.
